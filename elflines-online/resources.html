Progression

Ranks 1–3+
Increase one STAT by one. Mandatory for your first three Ranks — you can't pick anything else until Rank 3, when your Title locks in.
Ranks 4–10+
Choose one: raise a (x2)-cost Skill by 2 (Evasion/Dance, Melee Weapon, Archery, First Aid/Paramedic/Surgery), or raise two different non-(x2) Skills by two each. No Stat or Skill can go above 10.
Combat & healing rules+
All characters can Evasion-dodge ranged attacks. Repairs take a minute and cost half the usual gp. Out of combat and outside Miasma, a stabilized character heals to full within a minute, and a quick-fixed critical injury counts as fully treated. None of that works inside Miasma.
Death & Miasma+
Hit 0 HP and you're teleported to your last camp, sacrificing a 2,000gp death tax. Can't pay it? You get Revive Sickness — MOVE drops to 1 until you clear the debt (or buy 2,000gp for 20eb). Land a killing blow on a non-Elfline-mate inside Miasma and you collect 1,000gp of their tax, even if they pay it off weeks later.

Interacting with other players

Outside of ELO's own game mechanics — haggling with a real person instead of a quest NPC, for instance — use your actual Skill base, not your character's in-game one. The distinction matters more than you'd think once real eb gets involved.
