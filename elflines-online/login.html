Don't have an account?

Elfline Accounts are provisioned automatically the first time your Segotari RUSH REVOLUTION headset connects to the Night City server — there's no separate web registration. If you're locked out, your account handle is tied to your headset's serial pairing, not this site. Lost the headset, lost the login. Contact your Elfline's officer if they can vouch for a recovery request.
