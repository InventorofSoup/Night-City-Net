About the game

What is Elflines Online?

Elflines Online is Segotari's launch title for the RUSH REVOLUTION interactive braindance headset — a full-immersion MMO where every player becomes an elf defending the last free settlements of the Elflands from the miasma and the monster hordes that came with it. No corp logo on your character sheet. No boss. Just your Elfline, your Title, and whatever's left of your paycheck after the subscription renews.

Guilds here are called Elflines, and they're less about loot splits than they are about the people who show up at 2am for a weekly reset because their real Elfline — the one with rent and a graveyard shift — can wait an hour.

At Rank 3, every character earns a Title based on how they built themselves: Sage, Bowmaster, Bladedancer, Quickhand, Warmheart, Wildblood, Windkin, Barkshield, Druid, or the jack-of-all-trades Wayfarer. Not sure which one fits you? Take the quiz.
