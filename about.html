Night City Net went up in 2043 as a personal bookmarks page and, against all odds, never went back down. Nobody's entirely sure who still pays for the hosting. Nobody's asked too hard.

WHAT THIS IS

An unofficial directory of things worth finding on the regional airgap — corp, indie, official, or otherwise. Sites get added when the maintainer hears about them, likes them, or loses a bet. There is no application form. There is no editorial standard. There is a webring, though, and that has to count for something.
