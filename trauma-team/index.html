COMMON QUESTIONS

Clear answers.
Important details.

For full terms, consult your member agreement and current regional coverage guide.

Does Trauma Team respond if I’m not a member?+

Our dispatch center can coordinate with municipal emergency services. Direct Trauma Team response and transport are benefits of active membership or a verified corporate agreement.

What happens if I cannot pay for the plan I want?+

Eligible applicants may be offered installment billing, employer sponsorship, or a lower coverage tier. Financing is based on a financial and biometric risk assessment.

Will a team enter a Combat Zone?+

Response to restricted areas depends on your plan, current tactical conditions, available personnel, and the dispatch commander’s assessment.

Does my membership cover cyberware damage?+

Emergency stabilization may include medically necessary cyberware intervention. Repair, replacement, upgrades, and compatibility issues are covered only when explicitly stated in your plan.

Can I add someone to my plan?+

Gold and Platinum memberships may offer dependent coverage. All additions require eligibility review and may change your membership rate.

MEMBERSHIP DISCLOSURES

The important
small print.

Coverage is not insurance. Membership provides access to contracted emergency services subject to the applicable agreement.

Response is conditional. Membership does not guarantee a response, specific response time, extraction, transport, treatment outcome, or survival.

Balances remain your responsibility. Charges beyond stated treatment credit, including non-covered services and facility fees, are billed to the member.

We assess conditions in real time. Regional availability may change without notice due to weather, infrastructure failure, civil emergency, hostile activity, or capacity.
