Public Alerts

Alerts are issued by the district on a rolling basis and are not centrally reviewed. High severity alerts are things you should probably act on. Medium and low severity alerts are things the City is aware of and has chosen not to act on.

Boil-Water Advisory — South Night City

Water main integrity in South Night City remains compromised. Residents advised to boil tap water for at least one minute before consumption. Advisory in effect since March. City Manager Garven Haakensen has not responded to repair requests.
