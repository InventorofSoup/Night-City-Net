Laws & Ordinances

District ordinances, citywide requirements, security-provider directives, and corporate-zone conditions may overlap or conflict with each other. Publication here does not guarantee enforcement, or a right to appeal. Full official scans require sign-in.

Citywide (applies everywhere with a functioning City Manager)

Identity and Address Confirmation A-ORD-001

Anyone requesting district service must provide a verifiable SIN, CIN, employer credential, property record, or approved temporary identity. Unverified persons may be detained only when an authorized provider accepts custody.

Enforcement
District Security Provider
Penalty
Service denial; 20–100eb processing assessment
Emergency & Contractor Corridor Obstruction A-ORD-002

Vehicles, vendors, gatherings, debris, and temporary housing may not obstruct a route designated by the district Security Provider, a utility contractor, Trauma Team, MAX-TAC, or the City Manager.

Enforcement
District Security Provider
Penalty
Immediate removal; vehicle or property impound; costs assessed
Utility Connection and Meter Tampering A-ORD-004

Bypassing a meter, reconnecting suspended service, or interfering with contractor sensors is prohibited even when no regular municipal service is available in the district.

Enforcement
Utility contractor
Penalty
Restitution plus 200–5,000eb; service blacklisting
Hazard Reporting and Public Nuisance A-ORD-006

Fires, collapses, unexploded ordnance, radiation, contagious disease, and uncontrolled drones must be reported. Filing a report does not create a response obligation.

Enforcement
NC CivicNet intake, routed to district provider
Penalty
50–500eb for failure to report; false-report fees apply

Corporate & Executive Districts (Charter Hill, Exec Zone, Pacifica Playground, Downtown)

Credentialed Zone Entry C-ORD-011

Entry beyond public corridors requires a resident, employee, client, delivery, or sponsored-visitor credential. Credentials may be scanned, retained, or revoked without public appeal.

Enforcement
Militech / Lazarus / district provider
Penalty
Removal, detention, and access blacklist
Compliance with Private Security Direction C-ORD-012 · Stakeholder Only

Lawful directions issued by the contracted security provider carry district authority while within the provider boundary. Recording a restricted operation may constitute interference.

Enforcement
Contracted security provider
Penalty
250–5,000eb; credential suspension
Aerial Device and Counter-Surveillance Control C-ORD-013

Unregistered drones, optics, signal repeaters, and mapping devices are prohibited above private roads, residential compounds, and executive facilities.

Enforcement
Contracted security provider
Penalty
Device destruction; 1,000eb minimum

Combat & Contested Districts (Old Japantown, South Night City, Little China)

Provisional Security Checkpoint Compliance L-ORD-021

Checkpoints recognized by the City Manager or district Security Provider may search vehicles, verify identity, and redirect movement. Recognition does not guarantee patrol safety or complaint review.

Enforcement
Kimen-Gumi / Scythe Security / Gold Dragons (district-dependent)
Penalty
Detention, confiscation, or denied passage
Occupied Structure and Salvage Claims L-ORD-023

Removing fixtures, power cells, structural steel, medical equipment, or cyberware from a marked occupied building is prohibited without a local claim token.

Enforcement
District Security Provider
Penalty
Restitution at replacement value; local ban
Open Flame and Ammunition Storage L-ORD-025

Open fires, ammunition loading, and fuel transfer are prohibited inside occupied cargo stacks, tenements, clinic corridors, and marked evacuation lanes.

Enforcement
District Security Provider
Penalty
Immediate seizure; community restitution

Underserved & Unadministered Districts (Santo Domingo, Rancho Coronado, Hotzone)

Emergency Curfew Publication X-ORD-031

A curfew may be announced by radio, posted notice, patrol loudspeaker, or contractor message. Failure to receive the notice is not an exemption.

Enforcement
Whichever provider is available that day
Penalty
Dispersal or detention where a patrol is present
Potable Water Distribution Control X-ORD-032

Resale, diversion, hoarding, or unauthorized connection to a marked potable source is prohibited. Household allocation is not guaranteed.

Enforcement
District provider, where assigned
Penalty
Container seizure; exclusion from next distribution
Unavailable Provider Enforcement Clause X-ORD-035

A law remains in effect when no municipal provider is assigned to a district. Residents may not compel enforcement, investigation, protection, or appeal based on the mere publication of the law.

Enforcement
None assigned
Penalty
No standard schedule; contractor rates apply if one shows up

Military-Controlled (NorCal Military Base)

Controlled Installation Entry M-ORD-041

No public right of entry exists. All persons, vehicles, cargo, and implants are subject to search, escort, tracking, and immediate removal.

Enforcement
NorCal Military Police
Penalty
Military detention or exclusion
Command Curfew and Emergency Orders M-ORD-045

The base commander may establish immediate curfews, shelter orders, communications blackouts, exclusion zones, and compulsory evacuation routes.

Enforcement
Base command
Penalty
Military custody; no municipal appeal

Note on active war-zone districts: Night City recognizes no functioning ordinance system, court, appeal process, fine schedule, or guaranteed law-enforcement jurisdiction within the Old Combat Zone boundary. Any posted rules, tolls, curfews, or confiscations there are imposed by local armed groups and carry no municipal legal status. See Public Records for district administration status.
