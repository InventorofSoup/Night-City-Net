HOW NIGHT CITY IS ADMINISTERED

Authority follows funding.

The City Council adopts citywide policies, but most essential services are delivered through district offices, security agreements, utility contracts, corporate partnerships, and emergency-provider coverage. A Council vote does not necessarily create a funded service obligation.
