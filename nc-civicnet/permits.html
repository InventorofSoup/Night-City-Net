Click a permit type to expand requirements and apply. All fees are non-refundable regardless of outcome. Processing times listed are targets, not guarantees, not even close.

Fee & Processing Estimator

Public estimate only, not a quote. District classification affects both fee and processing speed.

Business License Fee: 400eb

Required to legally operate any storefront, stall, or fixed business address within city limits. Not required in districts without an active City Manager, since there's no one to enforce it.

Processing time
60–90 business days
Requirements
Notarized proof of address, biometric ID scan, district manager sign-off (where applicable)
Renewal
Annual, fee increases 8% each cycle without published justification
Application received. Estimated processing time: 60–90 business days. Do not follow up before day 91.
Cyberware Modification Permit Fee: 250eb

Technically required for any cyberware installation above Municipal Threshold. Enforcement is inconsistent to nonexistent outside of Corporate Plaza districts.

Processing time
45 business days
Requirements
Ripperdoc certification of install, biometric baseline scan, Humanity self-assessment form
Note
Permit does not cover military-grade or restricted cyberware, which requires separate Militech sign-off
Application received. Estimated processing time: 45 business days. Most applicants proceed with installation before approval anyway. We know. We can't stop you.
Firearms Permit Fee: 500eb

Required for legal ownership of any firearm above small arms classification. Enforcement varies enormously by district Security Provider — see Public Records before assuming this permit means anything where you live.

Processing time
90+ business days
Requirements
Background check, in-person interview, psych evaluation waiver (optional but recommended)
Application received. Estimated processing time: 90+ business days. Given current backlog, actual time may exceed this range considerably.
Curfew Exemption Pass Fee: 120eb

Permits legal movement during municipal curfew hours in districts where curfew is actively enforced (currently: none, but the form still exists).

Processing time
21 business days
Requirements
Employer verification letter or medical necessity documentation
Application received. Note: curfew is not currently enforced in any district, so this pass is, at present, entirely symbolic. Fee still applies.
Street Vendor Permit Fee: 150eb

Required for any mobile food, goods, or service cart operating on public right-of-way.

Processing time
30 business days
Requirements
Health inspection (self-certified, no inspector currently on staff), location assignment
Application received. Self-certification accepted as submitted; no verification will occur.

Fees are payable in eddies at any Municipal Payment Kiosk, none of which are currently operational. Mail-in payment instructions are available upon request, though no one currently staffs the request line.
